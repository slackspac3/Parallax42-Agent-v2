# Legal Intelligence Caveats

They may be U.S. jurisdiction-specific and must not be treated as UAE, GCC, EU, or other jurisdiction-specific advice. The deterministic council remains the decision owner and accountable human legal/compliance reviewers must approve final use.

The reference corpus is intended for clause-risk comparison, legal issue spotting, and reviewer questions. It must not be used as jurisdiction-specific legal advice or as an automated legal approval.
