These PDFs are generated test fixtures for the Compliance Intelligence Agent. They contain fictional parties only and are not legal advice. Each generated PDF is at least 20 pages and includes long-form schedules, control matrices, evidence acceptance rules, and intentionally unresolved approval blockers.
