## Positioning

After CI/live verification, the submission can claim the online-first product demo, root `/run` wrapper, named-Compass intake/Node specialists, isolated Qdrant retrieval, Node-only policy authority, authoritative multi-turn state, and scoped PostgreSQL audit. It must not claim a public FastAPI evaluator, enterprise SSO/membership/RLS, immutable audit retention, arbitrary scanned-PDF OCR, Qdrant in every evaluator path, active Python CrewAI, or enterprise-grade decision assurance. Residual export, identity, retention, admission, and deployment gates remain explicit.
