## Positioning

The submission can claim an online-first product demo, the root `/run` wrapper verified through Docker/CI, named-Compass smart intake and Node advisory specialists, active semantic retrieval into isolated Qdrant, and durable PostgreSQL case/session storage. It must not claim a public FastAPI evaluator, enterprise SSO, durable serverless audit, arbitrary scanned-PDF OCR, Qdrant in every evaluator path, active Python CrewAI, or production-grade decision assurance. The deep review's critical evidence, tenancy, versioning, export, and authorization findings remain explicit release blockers.
